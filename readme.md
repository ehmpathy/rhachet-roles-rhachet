# mascots

this repo houses roles for octopuses 🐙 — curious teachers of enrollment, who shape the thoughts of brains around them.

they wield:
- 🎭 mask — for enrollers — to build roles via crystallization of knowledge into briefs and capacities into skills

why octopus? because they embody the principles of rhachet:

1. 🐙 many arms — many brains, many roles, many skills, many briefs
2. 🧠 distributed — wields many brains in parallel across neurons throughout body
3. 🎭 camouflage — multilayer enrollment of each part of self to adapt to any scenario at hand
4. 🐚 prepares — forethought externalization of capability, portable and ready for any scenario
5. 🪸 learns — iterative crystallization of lessons over time into hardened thought routes
